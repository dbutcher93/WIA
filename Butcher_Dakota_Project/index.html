About All Eyez

All Eyez was founded by 2 friends, Dakota and Frank, in 2013. We are a clothing company / design company. We originally wanted to be a skateboard company, but decided to widen our audience by focusing on clothing and design. We both have design backgrounds, Frank is a Graphic Designer and I am a Web Designer and I do a little Graphic Design. We decided that when we are not designing for clients we will design for our clothing company.
